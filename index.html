      How To Play

      A quick guide to trading, scouting, and growing your post.

      Core Loop

      Buy cargo at Sol Nexus, send merchant ships to discovered systems, sell outbound cargo there, buy return cargo, then sell it back home for profit.

      Finding Good Routes

      Use the Discovered Systems panel for the two best route suggestions, or open the full systems browser to sort by distance, profit, or profit efficiency.

      Fleet Management

      Merchant ships can be named, recolored, and upgraded in the Hangar. Cargo upgrades raise carrying capacity. Speed upgrades shorten travel time.

      Scouting

      Buy a scout ship in the Shipyard to reveal more systems over time. More discovered systems means more profitable route options.

      Corporate Contracts

      Three premium bulk-delivery contracts are always available. Deliver the requested commodity to the target system before the timer expires to collect a large bounty.

      Markets And Events

      Temporary news events shift local prices for a while. Structural events can permanently change a system’s market class or the galaxy-wide base price of a commodity.

      Funding Missions

      If you lack credits for a full route, you can launch a partial load or finance the trip. Financing borrows the shortfall and adds time-based interest.
